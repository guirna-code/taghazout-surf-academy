SURF LESSONS IN TAGHAZOUT

Learn, grow, and achieve your goals with our expert courses

Our Courses

Whether you want to take a surf lesson or join a guided stand-up paddle tour, Taghazout Surf Academy has you covered. Our mission is to help you ride the famous waves of Taghazout while enjoying the ocean and discovering the beauty of this iconic surf village. Our professional instructors will guide you step by step to ensure you get the most out of every session. All equipment is provided, so you can simply focus on learning, progressing, and having an unforgettable experience on the Moroccan coast.

Surf

Surfing is more than a sport .It’s a lifestyle that brings energy, balance, and connection to the ocean. Whether you’re a beginner discovering your first wave or an experienced rider chasing new challenges, surfing offers physical strength, mental clarity, and pure joy

Surf for everybody

Surfing is for everyone, regardless of age or experience. Our programs welcome beginners and seasoned riders alike, focusing on fun, safety, and learning at your own pace. With expert guidance, supportive coaching, and a positive environment, anyone can discover the joy of riding waves and develop confidence in the water.

Professionnal surf

Professional surf coaching to help surfers of all levels ride waves with confidence, improve technique, and enjoy a safe, exhilarating experience on the water.

Private surf lesson

A private lesson is the best option if you want more one-on-one time with the instructor. Private lessons are best for children between 5 and 14 years old, intermediate surfers that want to step it up, or even beginner surfers that require extra instruction

surf group

The perfect option for beginning your surfing career. Participants must be at least 14 years old and if you want to join a group lesson but are visiting on your own we’ll incorporate you to one. Perfect if you want to learn with friends

Semi private

Three people will be getting surf lessons with this option. No matter the skill level of any participant, your surf lesson will be adjusted to accommodate everyone and make sure instruction is accordingly
