Courses Lessons

Experience personalized surf coaching, expert surf guiding, and unforgettable ocean adventures tailored to every level. Whether you’re a beginner catching your first waves or an advanced surfer refining technique and performance, each session is designed to help you grow with confidence and style.
Based in Morocco, Taha Surf Coaching blends deep local knowledge, high-level surfing expertise, and a positive, motivating coaching approach. The goal is simple: help you surf with confidence, flow, and power—while fully enjoying the magic of the ocean.

Beginner

  • 2-hour surf lessons
  • Surfboard and wetsuit included
  • Small group: 1–6 people
  • Private lessons available (1–2 people)

Intermediate / Semi-private

  • 2-hour surf lessons
  • Video analysis for faster progression
  • Surfing different spots based on conditions

Advanced

  • 2-hour surf coaching sessions
  • In-depth video analysis and feedback
  • Surfing a variety of spots based on conditions
